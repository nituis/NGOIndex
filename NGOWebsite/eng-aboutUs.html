About Us & Contact US

Mission

Hong Kong has a number of NGOs, however, small-scale NGOs may not have enough capital to promote their organization. Hong Kong NGO Directory website aims at facilitating an overview of NGOs in Hong Kong, especially those organizations which is not well-known to the public, and presenting all basic information of NGOs in Hong Kong. Undoubtedly, it can increase public understanding of the non-governmental communities in Hong Kong in advance. What is more, it helps the public discover, explore and meet NGOs that are helpful to them.
