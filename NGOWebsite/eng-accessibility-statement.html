Accessibility Statement

Our website is written according to the World Wide Web Consortium (W.3.C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (W.C.A.G.) 2.0 Level AA requirements. Following these guidelines will make content accessible to a wider range of people with disabilities, including blindness and low vision, deafness and hearing loss, learning disabilities, cognitive limitations, limited movement, speech disabilities, photosensitivity and combinations of these. Following these guidelines will also often make our Web content more usable to users in general.

If you find any aspects of this website inaccessible or encounter any difficulties in use, please email us at support@gmail.com for assistance.
